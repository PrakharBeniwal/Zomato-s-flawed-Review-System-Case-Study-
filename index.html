Act 1: The Unified Era

The First Blackbox: Dine-in vs. Delivery

Prior to 2020, Zomato operated with a single, monolithic rating. The platform algorithm combined physical dine-in ambiance with third-party delivery logistics into one unified score, causing high-end restaurants to be artificially penalized for delayed riders.

Act 2: The Emergent Flaw

The Second Blackbox: Restaurant vs. Rider

Act 3: The Current Gap

The Unsolved Core: The Attribution Void

Zomato's solution was to split the UI into two sliders (Rate Food, Rate Delivery). But they achieved UI Decoupling without Attribution Decoupling. The end consumer is forced to guess whose fault it is, placing massive cognitive load on the user and generating contaminated telemetry data.

In practice

When a user receives cold food, they confidently blame the restaurant for poor packaging - entirely unaware that the platform's assignment algorithm held the order for 20 minutes during a rain surge.

Garbage data feeds a broken penalty system - while the real bottleneck stays hidden.

PM RIGOR · MEASUREMENT

Measuring Attribution Quality.

An engine that reassigns fault is only credible if its accuracy is measurable. These are the numbers the system is held to.

North Star Metric

Misattribution Rate

The share of fault-assignments where the engine's verdict overturns the naive user tag - the single number the entire system optimises toward zero. It captures the core promise in one figure: every order where blame would have landed on the wrong actor without the engine.

▼ Minimize corrected fault-assignments ÷ total fault-assignments

Adjudication Accuracy

Engine verdict vs. ground-truth telemetry on a sampled audit set - does the routed fault match what the timestamps and GPS actually show?

▲ Maximize

Unfair Strikes Overturned

Rider and merchant penalties reversed by the engine before they ever hit a public profile or payout.

▲ Maximize

Telemetry Coverage

Percentage of orders carrying complete timestamp + GPS data - the input-quality gate that determines how often the engine can rule at all.

▲ Maximize

Merchant / Rider Dispute Rate

Share of adjudicated verdicts formally contested by either party - the trust check on whether routed fault is accepted as fair.

04 / ECOSYSTEM DEFENSES

An engine is only as honest as its inputs.

Attribution truth is worthless if the signals feeding it can be gamed. Three protective guardrails orbit the core adjudication engine to keep it incorruptible.

Input Integrity

User Trust Scores

Every complaint is weighted by the reporter's own history. Serial complainers, refund-farmers, and accounts with abnormal dispute patterns have their tags automatically discounted - or quarantined entirely - before they ever reach the adjudication core. A single bad actor can no longer manufacture a strike against an honest rider or a clean kitchen.

Weighted signal → manipulation neutralised
Physical Compliance

Packaging Spec

Spillage and leakage are deterministic, not subjective. Merchants are required to use Hyperpure spec packaging for gravy-based and leak-prone items. When an order ships outside the spec and a "spilled" or "leaked" tag lands, fault routes to the kitchen automatically - no telemetry argument needed. The missing compliance is the proof.

Spec violation → deterministic fault
Accountable Settlement

Escrow Ledgers

Penalties and refunds never fire on a guess. Every strike and payout is held in escrow until the engine returns a final verdict, then fault and finance settle inside the same atomic transaction. Money only moves once the truth is established - so when a verdict is reversed, the rupees reverse with it. No orphaned penalties, no premature payouts.

Held in escrow → settles atomically
05 / THE MONETIZATION MASTERSTROKE

Algorithmic Triangulation.

Once fault is routed accurately, the data stops being a liability and becomes a sales pipeline. A 1-star complaint is historically a sunk cost. But when 5 users report [Spilled] across 5 different riders for the same restaurant, rider error is statistically ruled out.

The engine mathematically flags the merchant's cheap packaging as the root cause - and automatically generates a zero-CAC B2B Enterprise sales lead for Zomato Hyperpure spill-proof containers.

The same telemetry that shields a rider's livelihood quietly closes a Hyperpure B2B deal. Trust infrastructure becomes a high-margin revenue engine.
