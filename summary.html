03 · The Solution

Intercept the tag. Route the fault.

Hold the user's tag before it hits a public profile. Cross-reference it against hard telemetry, timestamps, GPS and kitchen logs, then route the strike to whoever actually caused the delay.

A clean one-tap rating for the customer. The truth for the system.
